NEW YORK STATE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR
NOTICE OF THE 2027 NEW YORK STATE AVERAGE WEEKLY WAGE

Pursuant to Workers' Compensation Law section 2(16), the Superintendent of
Financial Services has determined the New York State Average Weekly Wage
(NYSAWW) applicable to Paid Family Leave benefits.

Effective January 1, 2027, the New York State Average Weekly Wage is
$1,925.14.

Accordingly, for leave commencing on or after January 1, 2027, the maximum
weekly Paid Family Leave benefit is 67% of the NYSAWW, or $1,289.84 per week.
The benefit rate of 67% of the employee's average weekly wage, and the maximum
duration of 12 weeks, are unchanged.

Employers and carriers should update payroll and claims systems accordingly.
