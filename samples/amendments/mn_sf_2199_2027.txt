STATE OF MINNESOTA — NINETY-FOURTH SESSION
S.F. No. 2199 — AS ENACTED

An act relating to paid family and medical leave; modifying benefit duration
and eligibility; amending Minnesota Statutes, chapter 268B.

Section 1. Minnesota Statutes, section 268B.04, is amended to read:

  The maximum combined duration of family and medical leave benefits in a
  single benefit year is increased from 20 weeks to 24 weeks, effective for
  benefit years beginning on or after January 1, 2027.

Section 2. Minnesota Statutes, section 268B.09, is amended to read:

  Employment protection under this chapter applies to an employee who has been
  employed by the current employer for at least 90 days, PROVIDED THAT for
  employers with fewer than 10 employees, employment protection applies only
  after 120 days of employment. Effective January 1, 2027.

Section 3. EFFECTIVE DATE. Sections 1 and 2 are effective January 1, 2027.
